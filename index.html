Points Get

See the tumblr post about this game.

While a squid is alive, it earns points. The first team to fill their bar wins.

Press combat to have one of the orange squids fight with the blue squids. There's an equal chance of the following outcomes:

  • You get splatted, and splat 0 blue squids.
  • You get splatted, and splat 1 blue squid.
  • You get splatted, and splat 2 blue squids.
  • You get splatted, and splat 3 blue squids.
